about knixtrack

We are knixtrack. A period tracking app for teens between the ages of 12 and 17. Knixtrack was founded by four teens, Dhwani, Sanaa, Naomi and Lee-Anne. That is where one of our slogans, 'By teens, For teens', came from. Currently, our main target audience is teen girls in North America but we hope to expand that target by next year.

We hope to inspire young girls to be the boss of their own period through this app.

This app includes many features such as an access to a gynecologist, a forum to interact with other teen girls, a personalized calendar feature and information on hygiene.
